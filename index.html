Zezhou Liu

Hi! I am Zezhou Liu, a post-doc fellow working in Prof.Reisner's Lab at the Physics department of McGill Univerisity. As a physicist, I'm passionate about exploring the behavior of polymers under different nanoscale confinements and advancing nanofluidics technology to enable single-molecule single-cell level analysis. Currently, I'm using solid-state nanopore technology to extract structural information encoded in different biopolymers. During my Ph.D. study, I studied the multiple ds-DNA interaction under a nanoscale confinement and the biological implications that coming from our in vitro experiment. You can see my publications down below.
